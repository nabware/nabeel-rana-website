About Me

Pivoting from Business Operations to Software Engineering

My name is Nabeel and I’m a software engineer, but I wasn’t always. During my two years as an operations analyst in the server industry I developed Excel macros and worked with software engineers to improve internal processes. This rekindled my excitement for software and I decided to leave to build my own software product. I wanted to build engaging gaming experiences similar to the games I played growing up.

During my five years as a software developer I built working prototypes for a Roblox-like gaming platform using a game engine and cloud microservices. This included procedurally generated terrain, custom shaders for texture blending, and dynamic animation. I eventually realized that building this platform was too complex for one developer and decided to look for opportunities with other companies. I completed a full stack coding bootcamp to enhance my self-taught experience with a holistic understanding of modern software engineering.

I want to continue my passion for building engaging games by working with game programmers and graphic artists. I bring the ability to learn on the fly and perseverance to handle the complexity of game development.
